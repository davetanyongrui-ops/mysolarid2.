The Shared Savings Model (SSM)

"Zero Upfront Cost. Shared Success."

The Shared Savings Model (SSM) is designed for those who want the financial and environmental benefits of solar energy without the initial capital expenditure. This is a true partnership model where our success is directly tied to yours.

How it Works:

  • check_circle Zero Investment: We handle the entire cost of the equipment and professional installation. You pay $0 to get the system up and running on your roof.
  • check_circle Immediate Savings: Once activated, the system immediately begins generating clean electricity from the sun, instantly reducing your monthly utility bill.
  • check_circle The 50/50 Split: The monthly savings (the difference between your old energy costs and your new, lower bill) are shared equally. You keep 50% of the savings as immediate extra cash flow, and the remaining 50% goes toward covering the equipment and our services.
  • check_circle Risk-Free Performance: Because we only earn when you save, we are highly motivated to ensure your system always performs at its absolute peak.

Why Choose SSM?

  • task_alt Preserve your capital and cash flow for other priorities.
  • task_alt Enjoy complete peace of mind with no hidden fees or maintenance worries.
  • task_alt Start saving money from day one with zero financial risk.

Direct Ownership (Outright Purchase)

“Asset ownership. Maximum ROI."

For those who prefer to own their assets and keep 100% of the benefits, our Direct Ownership model is the most straightforward path to true energy independence.

How it Works:

  • check_circle Outright Purchase: You pay for the system and installation upfront. Once the project is complete, you own the hardware entirely.
  • check_circle 100% Savings: Every kilowatt-hour of energy produced by your solar panels belongs to you. You keep 100% of the savings generated by the system for the entire lifespan of the equipment (typically 25+ years).
  • check_circle Asset Appreciation: Installing a solar system is a powerful investment. Direct ownership instantly increases the market value of your home or industrial facility.

Why Choose Direct Ownership?

  • task_alt Achieve the fastest possible payback period.
  • task_alt Unlock the highest long-term financial return on your investment.
  • task_alt Maintain full control over your energy infrastructure and future upgrades.

Which Model is Right for You?

Choosing the right path ultimately comes down to your financial goals. If you want to lower your energy bills immediately without opening your wallet, the Shared Savings Model is your perfect match. If you have the capital ready to invest and want to maximize your lifetime financial returns, Direct Ownership is the way to go.
